# IO FIT

**Developed by:** Ori Arad and Itamar Cohen

## Introduction

**IO FIT** is an innovative fitness app designed to revolutionize your workout experience. With a robust set of features, IO FIT helps users track progress, discover exercises, and connect with a like-minded fitness community. Whether you're a seasoned athlete or just starting your fitness journey, IO FIT is here to support you every step of the way.

---

## Features

### Workout and Progress Tracking
- Create personalized **workout plans**.
- Start and complete workout plans while tracking your **history** and **progress**.
- View **graphs** of your progression over time.
- Keep track of your **training history**, **achievements**, and general data on your workouts.

### Exercise Library
- Search from a **400+ exercise library** for instructions and demonstrations.
- View **GIFs** that show how to perform exercises correctly.
- Use the app's **screenshot detection** feature to identify gym machines and receive tailored exercise suggestions for that machine.

### Personal Profile
- Maintain a personal page with:
    - Personal data (e.g., height, weight, body fat percentage).
    - Certifications (if applicable).
    - Social network links.
- Track and update your fitness stats as you progress.

### Social Features
- Share workout plans with the community and **vote** for others' plans.
- Post invitations for **personal training sessions** or join discussions.
- Engage with other fitness enthusiasts and exchange tips.

## Technologies Used

The app is built using the following technologies:

- **Client**: Flutter, Retrofit, FlutterFlow
- **App Server**: Node.js, Express.js
- **User Data Database**: MongoDB Atlas
- **Accounts Database**: Firebase
- **ML Server**: Flask, Python, PyTorch

Refer to the architecture diagram in the repository for a better understanding of the app's structure.

## Requirements

To run the application, you need the following:

- **Flutter SDK**: Version 3.19.3
- **Dart SDK**: Version 3.3.1

- **Python**: 3.x
